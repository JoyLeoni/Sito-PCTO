Work-Based Learning – 4th Year

Dublin Experince – School Year 2023/2024

We attended lessons at CES Coppinger Row.
This allowed us to improve our language skills and immerse ourselves in Irish culture.
In addition to the lessons, we had the opportunity to interact with our "host parents" in their family homes.
The interactive activities proposed by the teachers made learning more engaging and stimulating.

We travelled around Ireland to better understand the local lifestyle
and its territory.
Each stage of the journey offered us breathtaking landscapes, from the Atlantic coast to the green hills of the countryside.
We visited historical sites, castles, and small villages, directly experiencing local traditions and the warm Irish hospitality.
